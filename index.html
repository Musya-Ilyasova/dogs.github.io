What is DogGo?

DogGo is a Web3 lifestyle app with the elements of Game-fi and De-fi

  • Users own NFT-dogs, which they can walk to earn tokens, as well as upgrade, train and mate with other dogs to create new ones.

Our mission

Now everyone can get a virtual pet and even earn money by walking!

  • Healthy lifestyle for all

    It is known for a long time that dog owners havelower heart disease rates, as well as other health benefits.It is often connected to the need to walk your pet.

  • Low learning curve

    Players don’t have to buy NFTs to start earning money. Rent feature allows them to participate without initial investments!

  • Immersion into Web3

    Players get familiar with Web3 technologies while playing. Widespread promotion of Web3 is an important part of our mission!
